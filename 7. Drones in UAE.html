DRONES IN UAE

Drones used by Police

Dubai and Sharjah Police have deployed drones with loudspeakers to warn residents to stay at home to reduce the spread of coronavirus.In a video posted by Sharjah Police on its social media, an officer flies a device over crowded junctions and on the city's waterfront.The messages are broadcast in Arabic, English and Urdu.
